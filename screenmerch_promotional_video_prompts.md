# ScreenMerch Promotional Video - Artlist AI Prompts

## Overview
This document contains 10-second video prompts for creating a ScreenMerch promotional video using Artlist AI video generator. Each prompt corresponds to a specific image and creates a 10-second segment.

---

## Segment 1: Homepage Introduction (10 seconds)
**Image:** Homepage showing product categories
**Prompt:**
```
"Welcome to ScreenMerch the revolutionary platform that transforms your favorite video moments into custom merchandise. Browse our video homepage and discover endless possibilities to create unique products from your video content. Choose from multiple product categories including apparel, accessories, pets and more!"
```

---

## Segment 2: Video Selection (10 seconds)
**Image:** Play Video page with DJ Panda
**Prompt:**
```
"Start by selecting any video from our extensive library. Watch your favorite content come to life in our high-quality video player. Whether it's a DJ performance, an animated character, or any creative content, ScreenMerch makes it easy to turn video moments into merchandise."
```

**Image:** Select Screenshots page with multiple Panda screenshots
**Prompt:**


## Segment 4: Product Category Selection (10 seconds)
**Image:** Product category selection (Women's, Men's, Kids, Mugs, Hats, Bags, etc.)
**Prompt:**
```

```

**Image:** Selected screenshot (Panda image)
**Prompt:**
```

```

## Segment 6: Advanced Tools - Cart & Editing (10 seconds)
**Image:** Cart page showing tools (feather edge, corner radius, frame, double frame)
**Prompt:**
```
"Customize your design with professional editing tools. Add feather edges for smooth transitions, apply corner radius for rounded designs, add frames with single or double borders. ScreenMerch gives you complete creative control over your merchandise."
```

## Segment 7: Tool Page - Final Customization (10 seconds)
**Image:** Tool page with all customization options
**Prompt:**
```
"Fine-tune every detail on our comprehensive tool page. Adjust colors, add effects, crop images, and perfect your design. With ScreenMerch's advanced tools, your vision becomes reality. Create professional-quality merchandise in minutes."
```

## Segment 8: Checkout & Order Completion (10 seconds)
**Image:** Checkout page or order confirmation (if available, otherwise use a generic checkout/cart image)

```
"Once you're happy with your design, simply click checkout to complete your order. ScreenMerch handles printing, quality control, and shipping directly to you or your customers. Your custom merchandise is just one click away."
```

## Tips for Artlist AI:
- Use smooth transitions between segments
- Maintain consistent pacing (2-3 seconds per key point)
- Emphasize "ScreenMerch" brand name in each segment
- Focus on the ease of use and professional results
- Highlight the variety of products and customization options
